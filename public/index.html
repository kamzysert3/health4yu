How Can You Help?

1

Financial Support

Make a tax-deductible donation to support the construction of our hospital in Ghana and help us provide sustainable healthcare solutions.

2

Time & Equipment

If you have free time to share, help us gather medical equipment and coordinate its shipment by container to Ghana. Every piece helps save lives.

3

Medical Equipment Donation

If you operate a clinic or health facility, help us with valid, usable medical equipment and furnishings. Your retired equipment could be life-saving elsewhere.

4

Fundraising Support

Help us with our fundraising efforts. Email us at contact@health4yu.de about organizing fundraising events or campaigns for the hospital.

5

Professional Skills

If you have any other knowledge or capacity that can help us (IT, medical, construction, engineering, transportation, etc.), get in touch! Every skill matters.

The Power of Volunteering

Volunteers play a very meaningful role in the work of our organization. They support us with their valuable ideas, impulses, donations, humanitarian operations and assume representational tasks as ambassadors.

Their voluntary engagement and civic work keeps our organization in motion and contributes to the improvement of the quality level of our organization.

Your voluntary assistance is also very much needed. Please contact us via email with your name and contact number. We will soon get back to you.
